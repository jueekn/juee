Research

Functional Connectivity Reliability Analysis
Computational Memory Lab · University of Pennsylvania
Jun 2025 - Present

Asking how trustworthy brain-connectivity measurements using different well-known metrics actually are during memory tasks, using intracranial recordings from 383 epilepsy patients. The finding: many hypothesized "memory-specific" network connections don't reliably reproduce at the sample sizes labs typically work with.

Presented at CNS (2026) and CEMS (2026).
Tools Python iEEG time-series Statistical resampling
Inter-brain Synchrony in Mice
Ma Lab · University of Pennsylvania
Sep 2024 - Present

Measuring how the brain activity of socially interacting mice synchronize across frontal and olfactory regions. I built automated behavior-tracking pipelines from video and neural recordings, then ran causal analysis across frequency bands to map how brain regions influence one another. Assisted in making and implanting electrodes, Miniscope imaging, and single-unit activity tracking.

Presented at ACHEM-S (2025, 2026).
Tools Detectron2 MATLAB · HMMs Causal analysis
Computational Modeling of Deep Brain Stimulation
CNS Lab · IIT Madras
Aug 2023 - May 2024

Modeled how deep-brain-stimulation current spreads through tissue. I built an FEM solver driven by diffusion-imaging conductivity and coupled it to a spiking-neuron model, so stimulation settings could be tested in silico before ever reaching a patient.

Presented at Society for Neuroscience (2024).
Tools FEM solver DTI · conductivity Spiking models
Wrist Movement Classification from EMG Signals
BMD Lab · Hokkaido University
Jun 2023 - Aug 2023

Built an end-to-end pipeline to classify wrist movements from raw muscle signals. Engineered time- and frequency-domain features and trained a classifier that reached 91% accuracy across four gestures.

Published at IEEE IATMSI-2024.
Tools EMG Feature engineering Random Forest
Lung Cancer Nodule Identification
Periwinkle Technologies
Apr 2023 - Jun 2023

Benchmarked object-detection models for spotting lung-cancer nodules in CT scans, weighing accuracy against speed and model size to recommend what would actually work for low-resource screening settings.

Tools YOLOv5 Faster R-CNN Medical imaging
