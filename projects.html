ECoG-Based Finger Flexion Prediction Using Ensemble Learning

Spring 2025

Predicting continuous finger flexion from electrical activity recorded on the cortex, across three subjects. Engineered spectral and motor-related features over lagged time windows, then stacked per-finger gradient-boosted models under a single meta-learner with adaptive smoothing, achieving a 20% gain in decoding accuracy.

Turning live attention activity into music

Spring 2026

A real-time system that decodes focus and arousal from live-streamed scalp EEG and uses them to steer an adaptive music playlist. I led the signal side: robust live feature extraction, linked to a haptic wristband that nudges the listener with vibration, closing the neurofeedback loop.
