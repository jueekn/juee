Decoding finger movement from the brain's surface

Spring 2025

Predicting continuous finger flexion from electrical activity recorded on the cortex, across three subjects. I engineered spectral and motor-related features over lagged time windows, then stacked per-finger gradient-boosted models under a single meta-learner with adaptive smoothing — reaching strong correlation on held-out data.

Turning live brain activity into music

Spring 2026 · Signal Processing Lead

A real-time system that reads focus and arousal from scalp EEG and uses them to steer an adaptive music playlist. I led the signal side: robust live feature extraction, plus a link to a haptic wristband that nudges the listener with vibration — closing the neurofeedback loop.
